About the Event

Charity, Fitness & Fun — A Happy Ramadhan for All

🎯 Vision

To create a sustainable community initiative that combines fitness, charity, and spiritual fulfillment during the blessed month of Ramadhan.

💝 Mission

To bring together communities in support of those in need while promoting health, wellness, and the spirit of giving during Ramadhan.

🎯 Impact

Our goal is to feed 1,000 families this Ramadhan. Through your participation and donations, we can make a Happy Ramadhan for All!
